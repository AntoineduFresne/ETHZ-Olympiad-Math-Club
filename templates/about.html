About Us

Welcome to the Math Olympiad Club!

We are a group of passionate mathematicians dedicated to solving challenging problems and exploring the beauty of mathematics. Our club focuses on preparing for and participating in math competitions, especially this year the International Mathematics Competition for University Students (IMC) competition. But in long term we aim to do better and participate to other competition.

Our mission is to foster a love for mathematics, encourage problem-solving skills, and provide room for students to meet and to challenge themselves with olympiad style problem. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced problem-solver, we hope you'll find something in our meeting to inspire you.

Join us as we tackle interesting problems, share solutions, and learn from each other. Let's explore the world of mathematics together!

Our Goals

  • Develop problem-solving skills
  • Prepare for math competitions
  • Foster a love for mathematics
  • Encourage collaboration and learning
